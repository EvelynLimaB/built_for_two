# 🌸 BUILT FOR TWO

> *Two sisters. One past. Every wrong choice.*

**Made for the [Toxic Yuri VN Jam 2](https://itch.io/jam/toxic-yuri-vn-jam-2)**

---

## 🖤 THE VIBE

**Daisy** is a ghost who pays rent. She codes, she doomscrolls, she hugs her plushies at night. She survived something that should've killed her, but *surviving* and *living* are two very different things. She's terrified of men, terrified of intimacy, and *terrified* that the only person she's ever really loved will look at her and see a monster.

**Belle** is a runaway who's been running so long she forgot what she was running from. Or maybe she remembers too well. She survives on charm, lies, and a body she learned to weaponize before she understood what that meant. She knows she's broken. She knows she's self-destructive. She *knows* she's making everything worse. She just can't stop.

When Belle shows up at Daisy's door after a year of silence, neither of them is ready. They're both drowning. They're both desperate. And neither of them knows how to ask for the one thing they need.

---

## 💀 WHAT THIS GAME IS ACTUALLY ABOUT

This is a story about two people who are *convinced* they're the worst thing that ever happened to the other person. It's about drowning together and mistaking each other's hands for anchors. About the ways trauma makes you reach for the people you love and *squeeze* until they break. About being so scared of losing someone that you push them away first.

**Every single choice they make is the wrong one.** But they keep making them anyway. Because that's what trauma does. That's what love does. That's what happens when you've never been shown how to do it right.

The game is about how these two stupid girls somehow make every single wrong fucking choice and use their own trauma to make the other's worse. It's about the ways we hurt the people we love because we don't know how to let ourselves be loved.

## 💕 TOXIC YURI?

This game was created for the **[Toxic Yuri VN Jam 2](https://itch.io/jam/toxic-yuri-vn-jam-2)** – a jam for girlthings who make each other worse.

We love it when girls suffer, yaaaay!

**Built for Two** is a toxic yuri story because:

- Daisy and Belle *cannot* save each other, but they keep trying anyway
- Their trauma keeps resurfacing in ways that trigger each other
- They love each other desperately, but they don't trust that love
- Every attempt to heal opens up a new wound
- They're both convinced they're the villain in the other's story

## 👥 THE CAST

### Daisy Heart
- **Age:** 24
- **Pronouns:** she/her
- **Occupation:** Programmer (the "sits in the dark and codes" kind)

Daisy is a transfem programmer who spends most of her time working, doomscrolling, and avoiding the world. Severe bottom dysphoria? Check. cPTSD? Check. Resting bitch face she can't control? Double check. The only things she truly loves are her plushies, her younger sister Belle, and her ungodly modded games. She hasn't had an orgasm since she was raped at 17. She's terrified of becoming like the man who hurt her.

### Belle Heart
- **Age:** 18
- **Pronouns:** she/her
- **Occupation:** Unemployed (formerly homeless, currently menace)

Belle is Daisy's younger sister. She ran away from home at 17, spent a year couch-surfing and getting used, and developed a habit of hypersexualizing herself to get what she wants. She neglects her hygiene, doesn't cook, and can't stop making jokes at the worst possible moments. She blames herself for what happened to Daisy and believes she *deserves* every bad thing that happens to her. She's convinced she's the villain. She's also convinced she's not worth saving.
